Rev 5:9-10; Rev 7:9-10

Tribe & Tongue is a global platform that exists to unify the body of Christ from all nations, tribes and tongues as one global Church to bring praise and worship offerings to the King of Kings and Lord of Lords.

TRIBE & TONGUE Vision

Tribe & Tongue is a global platform that exists to unify the body of Christ from all nations, tribes and tongues as one global Church to bring praise and worship offerings to the King of Kings and Lord of Lords.

TRIBE & TONGUE Mission

The mission of Tribe and Tongue is to create a platform for artists from across the world to collaborate, learn, grow and empower each other. We want to build a community of like minded believers who will hold each other's hands just like Aaron and Hur who held the hands of Moses to lift the banner of Jesus high and bring glory to His Name.

Just as the Scripture says in Rev 7:9,10 that a multitude of believers from every nation, tribe, people and language will stand before the Lord crying out “Salvation belongs to our God who sits on the throne and to the Lamb,” we believe that as the Name of Jesus is lifted high through this platform, He will draw people from every tribe, tongue and nation to Himself. We want to see salvation being poured out into the nations through “Tribe and Tongue.”

We want this to be a cross cultural, multi-lingual and diverse platform where artists can foster each other's spiritual gifts to make an impact not just on Christian communities but every community that is in need of Jesus. We believe that as the words from these songs echo in every room, building, community, neighborhood, street and city, the power that is in these words will touch the hearts of people and lead them to Christ.

Our prayer is to see praise and worship songs not being limited to just our homes and Churches but resonated in public places like restaurants, cafes, hospitals, schools, colleges etc.., We believe that God is on the move like never before and in these end times, there is an urgent need to spread the gospel to the perishing and music is one of those important channels through which people can know about Jesus.
